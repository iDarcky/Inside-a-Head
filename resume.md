# Product Management & Business Analysis Experience
*Contextualized for the "Inside a Head" Project*

**Project:** Inside a Head (Personal Digital Studio)
**Role:** Product Manager / Lead Business Analyst

## Product Strategy & Ownership
* **End-to-End Product Lifecycle:** Directed the concept, design, and technical delivery of "Inside a Head," a multi-page web platform acting as a digital studio and portfolio.
* **Vision & Roadmap Development:** Defined the North Star metric of the platform, positioning it as an evolving digital organism to transition from a personal garden to a collaborative agency space.
* **Agile Delivery & "Build in Public":** Championed transparent, iterative release cycles by documenting features, technical pivots, and product decisions in a public-facing "Logbook," driving continuous user engagement.

## Requirements Elicitation & Scope Management
* **Feature Pivoting & Prioritization:** Analyzed content consumption patterns to pivot the legacy "Writing" module into a structured "Shelf" module. Defined specific UI requirements (e.g., initial 5-item display, 10-item expansion, padded numbering) to optimize the browsing experience.
* **Technical Debt & Performance Optimization:** Conducted cost-benefit analysis on UI frameworks, making the strategic decision to deprecate JavaScript-heavy scroll animations (Lenis/GSAP) in favor of native CSS scroll-snapping, drastically improving mobile performance and accessibility.
* **CMS & Content Architecture:** Acted as the technical BA to map content models. Integrated Sanity CMS as a headless solution, enabling scalable, decoupled content management for the Logbook and Shelf modules.

## UX/UI Strategy & Design Systems
* **Design System Governance:** Established strict design constraints based on the Vercel Geist design system (Geist Sans/Mono fonts, high-contrast monochrome palettes, Lucide icons) to ensure a cohesive, minimalist aesthetic.
* **User Journey Mapping:** Designed intuitive full-screen, section-by-section navigation flows. Defined acceptance criteria for user preference persistence, successfully implementing cross-session light/dark theme toggles via local storage.

## Quality Assurance & Release Management
* **Automated Testing Strategy:** Defined quality standards and integrated end-to-end testing utilizing Playwright, ensuring critical user flows (navigation, CMS integration) pass rigorous validation prior to deployment.
* **Cross-Functional Tech Stack Alignment:** Selected a modern, edge-ready tech stack (Astro, React) to balance developer velocity with end-user performance metrics (fast load times, SEO optimization).
